OOTD

Web

For my Web Production II course we were given an assignment where we had to create a website that utilized a database. We had the freedom to choose our own themes and topics. I came up with OOTD, a site which allows fashion forward and not so fashion forward people to build daily outfits. I came up with the idea because of the trend on the internet in which people would post their outfits of the day (OOTD). These types od post also serve as style inspiration for others who may have a hard time building outfits with their own clothes. OOTD would serve as a digital wardrobe where users could mix and match their clothes before wearing it and realizing it's a complete mess. I know I've been late to occasions in the past just trying to figure out what to wear. Additionally, with the ability to log their OOTDs, users can potentially get more wears out of their clothes. I included a page on the site with tips on how to contribute to sustainable clothing.
