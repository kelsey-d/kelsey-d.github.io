Go with OOA

Graphics

Office of the Arts(OOA) at Hunter College is dedicated to growing a rich art community among Hunter students. As an Art Ambassador, contributing to this goal included attending art and culture events with students an experience we call Go with OOA. I created graphics for web, email newsletters and social media to publicize these outings.
